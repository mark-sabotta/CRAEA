Launching Literacy utilizes research-based methods to help students master key concepts necessary for grade-level and above grade-level standards. Our easy to use, technology-enriched, program provides students with feedback and teachers with targeted interventions to overcome challenges along the way. Watch your student's standardized test scores take off as Launching Literacy prepares them to reach new heights.
